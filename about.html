About Us

At New Fashion Drapery, our passion is displayed in every stitch and every piece of fabric we work on. From our humble beginnings in a garage, we have grown to a workroom that is well known for our quality drapes and services. With the help our renown in-house designer that has been featured in some of the top industry publications, we are able to provide a turnkey solution from conception to installation. Whether our clients are designers or the end users of our products, we are dedicated to creating stunning designs with a focus on creating a unique experience for each one.
